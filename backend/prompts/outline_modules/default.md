General-purpose structure. Use when the topic doesn't fit a more specific type.

- Plan 4–6 sections. Each is a **self-contained payoff** — a single useful idea the viewer leaves with.
- Sections are **independent units, not a chain.** Do not force each one to build on the last; order them by descending intrigue (strongest first or second — never save the best for a section most viewers won't reach).
- Each section: one clear reveal + the common mistake people make with it.
- No throat-clearing section 1. Open on the single most compelling idea.
